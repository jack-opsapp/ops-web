A — Rich ambient strip

Vertical column docked to the right edge, always visible. Each notification shows title + body + action. Scrolls to handle unlimited count.

You see everything, all the time. Field-visible. Canvas loses ~22% width.

B — Minimal marks, expand on demand

Thin vertical strip of tactical marks on the right edge. Hover reveals titles; click opens the full card inline. Persistent items are brighter/thicker.

Ambient awareness without chrome. Costs almost no canvas. Requires one action to read detail.

C — Summoned drawer

No ambient presence. Small bell/count icon in the topbar corner + keyboard shortcut (e.g. `N`) opens a right-side drawer overlaying the canvas.

Full canvas when not needed. Zero ambient awareness — you have to look to know.
